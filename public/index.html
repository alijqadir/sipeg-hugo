Mission

Positioning Pakistan through Knowledge, People Centric Policies and Global Insight. Empowering individuals, communities and institutions to make informed, evidence driven and future-focused decisions and policy choices.

Strategic Engagement

We convene scholars, practitioners, and policymakers to interpret Pakistan’s evolving strategic choices across security, economic, and cultural dimensions.

Applied Insight

Our research translates geopolitical analysis into actionable guidance for individuals and institutions shaping the region’s future.

Focus Areas

Core program areas mapping Pakistan’s strategic, economic, and societal trajectories.
