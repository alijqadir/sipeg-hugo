## Mission
Positioning Pakistan through knowledge, people-centric policies, and global insight. Empowering individuals, communities, and institutions to make informed, evidence-driven, and future-focused decisions and policy choices.

## Purpose
Shafqat Institute for Pakistan and Emerging Geopolitics (SIPEG) is a policy research center dedicated to advancing strategic insight on Pakistan’s role in a rapidly changing world. We focus on Pakistan’s culture, identity, economic, and strategic positioning amid shifting local and regional dynamics, and great-power competition involving China, the U.S., Russia, and India. Through interdisciplinary research, thought leadership, and stakeholder engagement, SIPEG equips citizens, society, and institutions with tools to navigate uncertainty and shape a forward-looking national agenda. We provide expert analysis on domestic political-economy and regional dynamics, global power shifts, and national development through research, training, collaboration, and dialogue.

## Pivoting Research & Policy
- Geopolitics & Regional Security  
- Trade, Investment & Economic Policy  
- Education, Curriculum & National Capacity  
- Culture, Identity & Diplomacy  
- Technology & Strategic Foresight  
- Advising, consultancy, trainings, collaborations & research  

SIPEG solicits research papers, policy briefs, working papers, discussion papers, and position papers under these pivot areas, and engages scholars/experts to contribute to the agenda.

## Our Game Plan & Strategy
- Targeted research that connects local realities to regional and global shifts.  
- People-centric policies that link education, identity, and economic resilience.  
- Partnerships with academia, industry, and public institutions for actionable outcomes.  
- Scenario building and strategic foresight to prepare for uncertainty.  

## Expertise & Services
- Independent research, policy briefs, and working papers.  
- Consultation and advisory on political risk management.  
- Trainings, executive education, and leadership workshops.  
- Facilitation of collaborations, dialogues, and stakeholder engagement.  

## Consultation & Advisory on Political Risks Management
- Country and sector risk scans tailored to Pakistan.  
- Governance, regulatory, and political-economy assessments.  
- Scenario planning for geopolitical shocks and regional volatility.  
- Briefings for boards, executives, and public agencies.  

## Why SIPEG Matters?
- Integrates culture, identity, and economics with geopolitics.  
- Bridges academic rigor with policy action.  
- Engages diverse stakeholders—citizens, practitioners, scholars, and institutions.  
- Keeps a forward-looking lens on technology, demographics, and climate.
